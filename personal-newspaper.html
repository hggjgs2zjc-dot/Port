On starting the paper

By You / Editor & Sole Contributor / Front Page

This is the first entry of your own newspaper — a place to file the week's real thoughts before they scatter. Replace this paragraph with whatever is actually on your mind today: a decision you're weighing, something you read, a plan taking shape.

Keep entries short enough to write in one sitting. The point isn't polish — it's a habit of noticing, dated and kept.
